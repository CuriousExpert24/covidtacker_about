What is CoViD-19?

COVID-19, also called the Novel Coronavirus, is the disease caused by the Coronavirus. First identified in Wuhan, China, COVID 19 has spread around the world and has infected almost all countries. It is highly contagious and poses a greater threat for people over 65 or people who have underlying chronic diseases
